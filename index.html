About

A researcher who ships.

I'm a 15-year-old ML researcher working across the University of Pennsylvania and the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia, where machine learning meets neuroscience. I build systems that read brains, distinguish tens of thousands of individual zebrafish, phenotype disease from facial movement, and translate sign language into expressive speech in real time.

Across four research roles and a stack of independent projects, I care about the same thing: turning models into tools that produce real biological signal, and actually shipping them. Outside the lab, I've raised roughly $56,000 for cancer research and help run a STEM nonprofit that's sent 1,500+ kits to underserved kids.
