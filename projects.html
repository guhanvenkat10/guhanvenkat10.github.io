RIBOSCOPE

In progress · targeting Nature Methods, ISEF 2027

Mechanistic interpretability of RNA foundation models

The first mechanistic-interpretability framework for RNA foundation models, training BatchTopK SAEs and cross-layer transcoders across four architecturally distinct models to recover biological circuits learned with zero labeled data.

  • 84 reciprocal feature pairs (Jaccard ≥ 0.5)
  • SAE EV 0.882 (RNA-FM) / 0.674 (ErnieRNA)
  • Validated on MALAT1, NEAT1, BACE1-AS vs. MPRA + DMS-MaPseq

EEG Seizure Prediction

Targeting Frontiers in Neuroinformatics, ISEF 2027

A cross-cohort domain-gap study

Trained seizure-prediction models on pediatric CHB-MIT EEG and zero-shot evaluated on adult Siena EEG. The finding: normalization strategy, not architecture, is the primary driver of cross-dataset generalization failure.

  • InstanceNorm recovered zero-shot F1 0.025 → 0.281
  • Matched domain-adversarial methods needing target data (p = 0.0005)
  • CNN / Transformer / GNN benchmarked bidirectionally
  • PyTorch
  • CNN
  • Transformer
  • GNN
  • CHB-MIT
  • Siena

NEXUS-α

Multi-modal frontier trading model

Tri-modal tokenization → Inverted-Mamba backbone

A tri-modal price tokenization pipeline (Chronos quantile bins, VisionTS patches, float embeddings) fused via cross-attention into an Inverted-Mamba backbone, with a 3-tier sparse-MoE router and a DQN execution agent trained via RL from market feedback.

  • 36.2% simulated annualized return on NQ futures
  • Sharpe 2.11 · 63.4% win rate · 8.3% max drawdown
  • +11.4% risk-adjusted alpha over 6 months
